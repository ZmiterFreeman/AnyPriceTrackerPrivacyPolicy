Privacy Policy

Last update: April 25, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the Application ("we", "our", or "the App") handles user data. It applies to the current version of the App published on Google Play.

1. Personal Data Collection

The App does not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or address.

There are no forms for registration, login, or user profile creation.

If you voluntarily contact the developer via email, your email address and message content will only be used to respond to your inquiry and will not be shared with third parties.

2. Data Collected Automatically

The App uses third-party services to help improve stability and user experience:

  • Firebase Crashlytics
    Collects crash logs to help identify and fix bugs.
    Data includes device model, OS version, and technical crash details.
    Google Privacy Policy
  • Amplitude Analytics
    Collects anonymized usage statistics (e.g., screen views, button clicks) for analytics and product improvement.
    No personal content or identifiers such as name or email are collected.
    Amplitude Privacy Policy
  • WebView
    The App allows users to open third-party websites. These websites may collect their own data as per their respective privacy policies.
    The App itself does not process or store any content from those pages, except for the URL and technical CSS selectors explicitly saved by the user.

3. Permissions Used

Permission Purpose
Internet access Required to load websites in WebView and send crash/usage data to analytics services.
Foreground/background access Required to periodically check for updates on saved pages.
Storage (if applicable) May be used to temporarily store minimal technical data such as saved URLs and selectors. No media or files are accessed.

4. Data Security

All transmitted data (e.g., analytics, crash logs) is encrypted in transit using HTTPS. No sensitive user data is stored on our servers.
