Privacy Policy

Last update: April 25, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the Application ("we", "our", or "the App") handles user data. It applies to the current version of the App published on Google Play.

1. Personal Data Collection

The App does not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or address.

There are no forms for registration, login, or user profile creation.

If you voluntarily contact the developer via email, your email address and message content will only be used to respond to your inquiry and will not be shared with third parties.

2. Data Collected Automatically

The App uses third-party services to help improve stability and user experience:

  • Firebase Crashlytics
    Collects crash logs to help identify and fix bugs.
    Data includes device model, OS version, and technical crash details.
    Google Privacy Policy
  • Amplitude Analytics
    Collects anonymized usage statistics (e.g., app installs, app updates, app sessions) for analytics and product improvement.
    No personal content or identifiers such as name or email are collected.
    Amplitude Privacy Policy
  • WebView
    The App allows users to open third-party websites via an embedded WebView. If the user chooses to save a specific product page for tracking, this data — such as the page URL and related technical details — is stored exclusively on the user's device. The App does not actively collect or analyze any content from visited websites, and no such data is transmitted to the developer or any third parties.

3. Permissions Used

Permission Purpose
android.permission.INTERNET Required to access third-party websites and retrieve data when checking for price changes.
android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE Used to check if an internet connection is available before attempting to access web pages that the user has selected for price tracking.
android.permission.POST_NOTIFICATIONS Used to send notifications to the user when a tracked price changes.

4. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with the updated date.
